About Us.

Prohealth Community Care Services is an organisation that is committed to providing person-centred care based on individualised care plans that recognise the uniqueness of each person. We specialise in providing the highest quality care for individuals with a disability, as well as older members of the community. Our highly professional staff deliver high standard community care services to our clients. With us, you know you or your loved ones are in safe hands.
